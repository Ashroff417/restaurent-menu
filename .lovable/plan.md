

# Water Street Haven Restaurant Website

## Overview
A warm, rustic-styled static website for **Water Street Haven Restaurant** featuring a text-based logo, an inspiring quote, and a beautiful book-style menu with animated page-flip navigation. The site will have Mediterranean/earthy tones throughout.

---

## Page 1: Hero / Landing Section
- **Restaurant name** "Water Street Haven" in an elegant serif font as the logo
- A warm tagline/quote (e.g., "Where Mediterranean Flavors Meet Home")
- Warm background with earthy colors (terracotta, olive green, cream tones)
- Smooth scroll-down arrow or "View Menu" button

---

## Page 2: Book-Style Menu
An interactive **book with page-flip animation** containing these pages/sections:

1. **Main Platters** – Original, Philly, Super, Shrimp, Fire Fajita, Cheesy (with prices & descriptions)
2. **Sandwiches** – Gyro Wrap, Falafel Sandwich, Chicken Souvlaki Wrap
3. **Mediterranean Appetizers** – Hummus, Baba Ghanoush, Za'atar Fries, Greek Fries, Grape Leaves, Tzatziki, Greek Salad, Caesar Salad
4. **Sides** – Pita, Fries
5. **Desserts** – Baklava, Kunafa
6. **Drinks** – Hibiscus Ice Tea, Canned Soda, Water

Each page will have a parchment/paper texture feel with warm typography. Navigation arrows to flip between pages.

## Design Theme
- **Colors**: Warm cream background, terracotta/burnt orange accents, olive green highlights, dark brown text
- **Typography**: Elegant serif for headings, clean sans-serif for body
- **Feel**: Cozy, Mediterranean, inviting — like walking into a warm restaurant
